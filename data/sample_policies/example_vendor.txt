ExampleVendor Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-22

ExampleVendor provides analytics and advertising services to publishers.
This policy describes how we handle data we receive when you visit a
site that embeds our pixel.

1. Data we collect

We collect device data (IP address, browser, operating system, screen
resolution, language) and behavioural data (pages viewed, time on page,
clicks, mouse movements) from publisher sites that embed our SDK. We
also collect any data the publisher chooses to share with us.

We retain this data indefinitely for our own product-improvement and
research purposes.

2. How we use the data

We use the data we collect to:

  - compute aggregate analytics for the publisher;
  - build interest profiles to improve targeted advertising;
  - sell aggregated audience segments to advertising partners;
  - share data with our parent company and corporate affiliates.

3. Sharing with third parties

We share your data with our advertising partners and affiliates as
described above. We may also disclose data when required by law.

4. Your rights

If you would like to opt out of advertising-based use of your data,
please contact us at privacy@examplevendor.com.

5. Retention

We retain data indefinitely unless you specifically request deletion.

6. Contact

For privacy questions, write to privacy@examplevendor.com.

Privacy Policy for Example.com

Last updated: 2026-04-15

We at Example.com value your privacy. This policy explains what personal
data we collect and how we use it.

1. Data we collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  - Account data: name, email address, hashed password.
  - Usage data: pages visited, search queries, clicks. We collect usage
    data only for the purpose of improving our service. We will not use
    usage data for advertising purposes.
  - Device data: IP address, browser type, operating system. We retain
    device data for 30 days and delete it thereafter.

2. Third-party services

We embed Google Analytics on our pages. Google Analytics receives the
device data listed above so it can compute aggregate site-traffic
statistics on our behalf. We do not authorise Google Analytics to use
this data for any other purpose.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, port, and delete the personal
data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, email
privacy@example.com.

4. Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active and
for 90 days after deletion. We retain device data for 30 days, as
described above.

5. Contact

For privacy questions, contact our Data Protection Officer at
dpo@example.com.
